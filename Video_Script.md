# Aitomic Flow — 90-Second Demo Video Script

## Screen Plan + Voiceover Script

---

**[0–5s] — Opening hook — Landing page hero**

> _Screen: Open `aitomicflow.com`, hero section visible, slow scroll down_

**Voice:**

> "Most teams run their operations through email threads, chat messages, and spreadsheets. Things get missed. Deadlines slip. Nobody knows who owns what."

---

**[5–10s] — Log in — Quick transition into the app**

> _Screen: Click "Log in", type credentials, hit Enter — dashboard appears_

**Voice:**

> "Aitomic Flow changes that."

---

**[10–22s] — Dashboard — Show MedGroup live data**

> _Screen: Dashboard with 41 triggered, 25 completed, 5 SLA breached cards. Slowly pan down to flow breakdown table._

**Voice:**

> "Managers get a real-time view of everything — active flows, completed tasks, SLA breaches, and who's holding things up. No spreadsheet. No status meeting required."

---

**[22–38s] — Flow Canvas — The wow moment**

> _Screen: Click "Flow Builder" in sidebar → open a published flow → canvas fills screen with nodes and connections_

**Voice:**

> "Building a workflow takes minutes. Drag and drop your steps, assign owners, set deadlines, add branch conditions — then publish. The system takes it from there."

---

**[38–50s] — Trigger a flow — Show it working live**

> _Screen: Click "Trigger" button on a flow → fill in a quick form → submit → see the new instance appear in the instances list_

**Voice:**

> "When someone triggers a flow, tasks are automatically assigned to the right people. Every step has an owner. Every deadline is tracked."

---

**[50–63s] — Task inbox — The user's daily view**

> _Screen: Navigate to `/tasks` → show pending tasks list with assignee names, due dates, step names_

**Voice:**

> "Each team member opens their inbox and sees exactly what they need to do — nothing more, nothing less. No confusion. No 'did you see my email?'"

---

**[63–73s] — Back to dashboard — SLA and bottlenecks**

> _Screen: Navigate back to Dashboard → highlight the SLA Breached card and Pending at Who table_

**Voice:**

> "And when something's overdue, you see it instantly. Who's the bottleneck, how long it's been waiting, and what needs escalating."

---

**[73–82s] — Pricing — Landing page pricing section**

> _Screen: Open `aitomicflow.com`, scroll to pricing cards — Free and Pro side by side_

**Voice:**

> "Free to start for small teams. Pro is twenty-nine dollars a month flat — up to fifty users, no per-seat pricing, no surprises."

---

**[82–90s] — CTA — End on the hero with button**

> _Screen: Scroll back up to hero, zoom slowly into "Start for free" button_

**Voice:**

> "Aitomic Flow. Your team always knows what to do next. Try it free at aitomicflow.com."
